## Things to note

### This site is a work in progress.

Pages will be added, reorganized, and refined over time.

See the [Change Log](/pages/change-log) for updates.

Some pages include one of the following labels:

| Label | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| PLACEHOLDER | The article is not written yet. The placeholder page outlines what will be covered when the full article is complete, so you know what to expect. |
| DRAFT | The article exists and is written but is still being edited and refined. The content is there; it's just not finished. |

### Bookmark the top-level URL.

Save [docs.amandamashburn.com](https://docs.amandamashburn.com) rather than bookmarking individual pages.

I'm prone to renaming and reorganizing files at the moment, which breaks page-level URLs. The top-level domain will always get you to the right place. Once the site structure stabilizes, individual page links will be safe to save.

### View on a desktop.

To get full use of navigation panes (for the site and within a page), view on a desktop or laptop.
