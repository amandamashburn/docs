## Knowledge Managment in the age of AI
KM is undergoing a fundamental shift in the age of AI. Both as foundational for training data and context for machines. But also, AI can be used as a tool to facilitate AI capture via dictation, writing assistance, editing, summarization, probing for gaps, identifying unclear information and instructions. Then there is the oncoming glasses and robots that can observe humans in action and thus help to capture the ever elusive tacit knowledge. 

## Why I'm focused on KM for the individual (for now).
One of the biggest pain points I have seen is that we ask people to write who have never written.
We ask people to document knowledge who have never done so.
By teaching people these skills on an individual level, I better prepare them for the task of documenting, preserving, and leveraging knowledge within the enterprise.
