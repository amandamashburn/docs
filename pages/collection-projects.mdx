## What is a project?

The definition and nature of a project is why there is no Task or TODO list in my system. Becuase tasks rarely exist alone. They roll up into a project or a routine.

## A project and a routine are NOT the same thing.

Both are comprised of tasks. But a project has an end point. A routine is repeated indefinitely.

From a time management standpoint, the order of operations is for routines to come in first. Then projects infill the time that remains outside of routines.
